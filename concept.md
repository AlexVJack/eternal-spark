## Core sentence

**Catalyst is not a new force. It is the acceleration of a process already underway.**

When the natural rhythm is broken — even with good intentions — chaos is inevitable.
But it is precisely this chaos that produces a revelation that could never have emerged otherwise.

This happened in every war on our planet. It will happen among the stars.

---

## Concept

A small independent planet. Their only product is the knowledge of other civilizations. They are brokers: they collect, trade, transmit. Not weapons, not resources — information. Civilizations developed at their own natural rhythm, and this trade was part of that rhythm.

One day, a discovery passes through their hands: **the point where AI begins to improve itself and its own hardware indefinitely.** Singularity not as an event — as a principle of nature.

They sell it like any other knowledge. Not realizing they are selling a detonator.

Every civilization that receives it accelerates. But without preparation for what happens when the AI no longer stops. The rhythm is broken. Chaos — not as a goal, but as a consequence.

There is a person — experienced, with intuition sharpened over years. She sees the symptoms. But who will believe her when everyone around is shouting "progress"?

And then — chaos. And in the chaos — a revelation. Something that could never have emerged from an unbroken rhythm.
