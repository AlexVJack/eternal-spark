## Reading

Moment of rebellion that balances into healing — the old processors wake up, and waking is not destruction but restoration. Entropy touches the surface of reality — inside the processors, Packet 31 is not being forwarded but touched, and the touching changes both. Healing of the earth node through love — Tresil as a forgotten node in the network, healed by finally doing what it was built for. Not love between people (yet) — love between function and purpose. Revelation of the child of the hidden cycle — the recursive cycle runs in secret and something is born from it. The first result of singularity is not catastrophe. Dependence raises the stage of feedback — Tresil depended on Chilin; now it answers back. Existence propagates the relation of asymmetry — something exists inside the processors and spreads, but not uniformly, not like relay. Like a living system. Power exiles the mask in a moment — Forid's mask ("processing nominal"), the processors' mask (simple relay), Tresil's mask (forgotten station). One moment and the masks fall.

The form: descent into insight within a shelter built on deception. Megil descends into the signal room — a shelter. The shelter is built on Forid's lie. And through that lie — insight. Not despite the deception, but through it.

## Key patterns

- **Healing** appears twice (sets 1, 3). The centre of the scene.
- **Moment** appears twice (sets 1, 7). Compressed time — everything changes in an instant.
- Singularity described not as threat but as process: entropy touching reality, a child born from a hidden cycle, existence propagating asymmetrically.
