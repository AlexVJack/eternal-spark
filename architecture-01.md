# Architecture Draw 01 — Parallel Line

**Date:** 2026-03-06
**Question:** Does the story need a parallel narrative line? If so — what shape?

---

## Draw

Void · Touch · Fatigue · Boundary

---

## What the symbols answered

Not a ship. Not action. Something that stands still.

A remote outpost of the corporation — at the edge of something. A new character, alone, who has been waiting a long time. The *Censig* is coming to him. He knows. He has known for a while.

- **Void** — the outpost is at the edge of nothing. Far from the center.
- **Fatigue** — he has been there long. The waiting is not fresh.
- **Boundary** — he stands on a line between something and something else. Not yet defined.
- **Touch** — the arriving ship is the first contact from outside in a long time.

---

## Architectural decision

Two lines:
1. Aboard *Censig* — Megil, moving toward a decision she hasn't made yet
2. The outpost — someone waiting, at a boundary, not knowing what the ship carries

They converge. When — unknown. How — unknown. The symbols don't say yet.
